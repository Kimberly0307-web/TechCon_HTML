History:TechCon started in 2010 as a small annual meetup for tech enthusiasts to discuss emerging trends and share ideas. Initially focused on software and hardware innovations, it quickly grew in scope and popularity. By 2015, it expanded to include global participants and topics like AI, biotech, and sustainable tech. Its workshops, keynotes, and exhibitions have since become a hub for groundbreaking ideas. Today, TechCon is a leading global conference shaping the future of

Mission:TechCon aims to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and drive the advancement of technology for a better future. By bringing together industry leaders, developers, and visionaries, TechCon provides a platform for sharing knowledge, exploring emerging trends, and creating solutions that address global challenges.
